Overview

Princess Too is a distinguished 48-meter luxury motor yacht built by the renowned Dutch shipyard Feadship in 1999. This exceptional vessel represents the pinnacle of yacht craftsmanship, featuring exterior design by De Voogt Naval Architects and interior design by Sam Sorgiovanni.

Originally known as Excellence II, Princess Too has undergone comprehensive refits in 2006, 2014, and 2017, ensuring she maintains the highest standards of luxury and performance. With her steel hull and aluminum superstructure, she offers both elegance and durability for discerning yacht enthusiasts.

The yacht accommodates up to 12 guests in 6 luxurious suites, including a master stateroom with Jacuzzi, one VIP cabin, and four twin guest cabins. A professional crew of 10 ensures an exceptional level of service throughout your voyage.
